SzopPrecz Stop wondering if shops are opened this Sunday!

About

“Szop Precz” (eng. Raccoon) is simple yet useful application for checking shops availability in Poland. Starting from March 2018, shops will be closed on some Sundays (enforced by law), which leaves a burden of remembering the schedule.

Enter “Szop Precz” — wether you’re a resident or just visiting Poland, it’s an easy way to check if shops are opened. You can easily check shops availability for current day and schedule notification for upcoming shops close downs.
